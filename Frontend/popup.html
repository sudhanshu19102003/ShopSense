“Summery” is an adjective that means something is suitable for or typical of summer. For example, you might describe a dress as “summery” if it is light and suitable for summer. Something that is “summery” might have qualities like sunshine, brightness, freshness, airiness, lightness, or a lack of stress. For example, a drink with a “summery” taste might be sweet, cold, and refreshing. The word “summery” was first used in the 15th century. It is pronounced “sŭm′ə-rē”.
